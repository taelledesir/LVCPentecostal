About LVC

THE NAME OF OUR CHURCH IS LVC PENTECOSTAL WORSHIP CENTER. LVC STANDS FOR “LES VOYAGUER CELESTE “ WHICH TRANSLATES “THE HEAVENLY TRAVELERS.” TO US , THAT MEANS AS SERVANTS OF THE LORD JESUS CHRIST , WE UNDERSTAND WE ARE NOT HERE FOR LONG. WE ARE GLADLY SERVING THE LORD UNTIL HE RETURNS FOR US. LVS WAS FOUNDED SINCE FEBRUARY OF 2014, WITH ONLY 14 MEMBERS. WE STARTED IN ONE UNIT (103) AND WITHIN 2 YEARS , THE LORD GIFTED US WITH ANOTHER UNIT(104). WE THEN COMBINED THE TWO UNITS , WHICH ALLOWS US TO HAVE THE AWESOME SANCTUARY THAT WE NOW HAVE. AS THE YEARS GO BY, WE HAVE BEEN GROWING CONTINUOUS. WE ARE GRATEFUL TO SAY WE STARTED AT 14 MEMBERS AND WE NOW HAVE 100 + members .
